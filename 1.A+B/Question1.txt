1. A + B Problem
---Problem Description

Your task is to compute the sum of two integers, a + b.

----Input Description

The input contains a sequence of integer pairs a and b, separated by spaces.
Each pair of a and b is given on a separate line.

----Output Description

For each input pair of a and b, output their sum on a separate line.
If the second pair of integers appears on the second line of input, their sum should also be printed on the second line of output.

----Sample Input
3 4
11 40

----Sample Output
7
51
